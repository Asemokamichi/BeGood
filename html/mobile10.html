Safe Space: It's time to talk
25 spots
Safe Space: The Time to Talk programme is designed for those who need psychological support, emotional help and just plain human attention. It is a safe and warm place where anyone can freely share their feelings, receive advice and support and feel heard. We recognise that there are difficult times in life when it is important to just talk to someone who will listen without judgement. Safe spaces are informal meetings in a comfortable and trusting environment, where volunteers with psychological experience and empathy are ready to help. There is no judgement, just support, understanding and help in finding solutions to difficult situations.

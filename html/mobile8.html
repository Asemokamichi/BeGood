Harmony Across Generations
25 spots
The Harmony Across Generations programme aims to create a warm atmosphere of friendship and understanding between generations through a musical concert. The event will bring together children from an orphanage and elderly people from a nursing home to enjoy live music, socialise and share positive emotions. We are looking for volunteers to help organise this special evening. If you are willing to donate your energy and time to bring joy and togetherness to others, we would love to have you on our team.
